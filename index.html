TaskPilot

Plan better. Execute faster. Ship with confidence.

TaskPilot is a guided task planning assistant that helps individuals and small teams break goals into executable steps and generate daily plans.

This public MVP runs entirely in your browser: tasks are generated with a local rule-based planner and stored in localStorage. It does not call external large language model APIs.
